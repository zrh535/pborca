As mentioned in January on the DW_Ripper Site,
I made a presentation about the PBL File Format
and I still do have not much time.

some of its features. The program was compiled with
PB 8.0.3.

Libtool follows the analysis, that I presented
at the PowerBuilder User Group Germany Meeting
in Hamburg (12 May 2003).
"PBL File Format - undocumented but not unexplored"
can be downloaded in a few days from the PBUGG website.

A quick ASCII-Filedescription will also be released soon.
